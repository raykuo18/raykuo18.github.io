**Improving Limited Supervised Foot Ulcer Segmentation Using Cross-Domain Augmentation Strategies.** Equal first author with Po-Han Huang. Published at IEEE ICASSP 2024.

A two-stage cross-domain augmentation method (*TransMix*) combining Augmented Global Pre-training on HAM10000 with Localized CutMix Fine-tuning on FUSeg. Carried out at Inventec AI Center in collaboration with Taipei Veterans General Hospital.
